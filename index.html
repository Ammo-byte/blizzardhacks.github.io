What is Blizzard Hacks?

Blizzard Hacks 2020 is an online hackathon that will be hosted on Discord from January 9 to January 10. Participants will be put into groups of 4 and aim to create a product/prototype that matches the pre-determined theme of the event. Submissions will be in the format of a video as well as any other supplementary attachments.

Where

Completely virtual on an online communication app called Discord

When

Saturday to Sunday
9-10 January

F.A.Q

  • Do I need programming experience?

    This hackathon is beginner friendly, meaning you need no programming experience! Your final submission does not require code!

  • What will I be learning in an online hackathon?

    The sky is the limit! You can learn programming and design tips from mentors or even improve your communication skills! It is also the perfect oppurtunity to connect with new people in a digital environment.

  • Who can participate?

    This hackathon is open to all high-school students in Peel District School Board from Grades 9-12.

  • Where can I find the theme of the hackathon?

    The theme will be revealed on the day of the hackathon so all teams have the same amount of time to create their project.

  • What can I win?

    The winning team will win a $200 gift card for VRPlaying Toronto. There will also be more oppurtunities to win monetary prizes throughout the night.

  • Do I have to pay a fee?

    Nope! Blizzard Hacks 2020 is completely free! What are you waiting for? Sign up!
